Top Technology-Themed Games that we have

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role-playing game set in an open-world, dystopian future. Developed by CD Projekt, the game immerses players in the bustling metropolis of Night City, offering a gripping narrative, advanced technology, and intense combat. With breathtaking visuals and a captivating storyline, Cyberpunk 2077 has become a landmark in the gaming industry.

Deus Ex: Mankind Divided

Deus Ex: Mankind Divided is a cyberpunk-themed action role-playing game. Developed by Eidos-Montreal, the game follows the story of Adam Jensen, a mechanically augmented protagonist, as he navigates a world on the brink of chaos. With a focus on player choice and immersive storytelling, Deus Ex: Mankind Divided provides a rich and thought-provoking gaming experience.

Watch Dogs: Legion

Watch Dogs: Legion, developed by Ubisoft, is an action-adventure game set in a near-future London. The game introduces a unique mechanic allowing players to recruit and play as any character in the open world. With hacking as a central theme, players embark on a thrilling journey to liberate London from an authoritarian regime. Watch Dogs: Legion combines innovation with a gripping narrative, making it a standout title.

Control

Control, an action-adventure game developed by Remedy Entertainment, delves into the mysterious and supernatural. Players take on the role of Jesse Faden, exploring the Federal Bureau of Control to uncover secrets and face otherworldly threats. With its unique storytelling, mind-bending environments, and telekinetic abilities, Control offers a surreal gaming experience that challenges both the mind and reflexes.

Portal 2

Portal 2, developed by Valve Corporation, is a puzzle-platformer that continues the story of Chell and the sentient AI, GLaDOS. With the iconic portal gun, players solve intricate puzzles that bend the laws of physics. The game's clever writing, innovative mechanics, and humorous dialogue have made Portal 2 a classic in the puzzle genre, leaving a lasting impact on players.
